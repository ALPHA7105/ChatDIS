11. Student Recognition & Awards

The school acknowledges and celebrates excellence in academics, conduct, leadership, and attendance through the following awards:

Scholar of the Year: Awarded to the top academic performer in each grade.

Proficiency Awards: Recognizing subject-wise excellence (e.g., Aryabhatta Award for Mathematics, Einstein Award for Science).

100% Attendance Award: Encourages consistency and punctuality.

Dunes Prince & Princess: Based on academic performance (90%+), co-curricular involvement, and a leadership interview.

Principal’s Special Award: Awarded for cohort-level excellence (Grades 3–5, 6–8, 9 & 11), requiring 90% marks and 95% attendance.
